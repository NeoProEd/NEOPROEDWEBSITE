Privacy Policy

NeoProEd Private Limited  ·  Effective date: April 2025  ·  Version 1.0

This Privacy Policy explains how NeoProEd Private Limited ("NeoProEd", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you visit our website or enrol in our programmes. We are committed to compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian law.

1. Who we are

NeoProEd Private Limited is a professional education organisation registered in India (NIC Code 85499). Our website is neoproed.com. For data-related queries, contact us at neoproed@gmail.com.

2. What personal data we collect

We collect only the data required for the stated purpose. Depending on your interaction with us, this may include:

  • Identity data: name, professional role, organisation name
  • Contact data: email address, phone number, city
  • Professional data: career background, programme interest, referral source
  • Payment data: Razorpay transaction ID, UPI reference, bank UTR number. We do not store card numbers or bank account credentials — these are handled by Razorpay and your bank respectively.
  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, device type — collected via Google Analytics 4 only after you give consent.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (health, financial records, biometric data) unless explicitly required for a specific programme and separately disclosed.

3. How we use your data

We use your data only for the purposes described at the point of collection:

  • Responding to your enquiry or application
  • Processing your enrolment and payment
  • Sending programme-related communications and updates
  • Improving our website and programmes (analytics, only with consent)
  • Legal and compliance obligations

We do not use your data for automated profiling or decision-making that produces legal effects.

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your data on the basis of:

  • Consent: for marketing communications and analytics tracking. You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing neoproed@gmail.com.
  • Contract: to fulfil your enrolment and deliver the programme you enrolled in.
  • Legitimate interests: to respond to enquiries and operate our business.
  • Legal obligation: to maintain records for GST and regulatory compliance.

5. Who we share your data with

We share your data only with service providers necessary to deliver our programmes and operate our website:

  • Learnyst: our student learning platform — receives your name and email to create your student account.
  • Razorpay: payment processing. Razorpay is regulated by the Reserve Bank of India and processes payments within India.
  • Zoho CRM: lead and enquiry management. Data stored in India.
  • Resend: transactional email delivery. Data stored in the US — we are monitoring DPDP data localisation rules and will switch to an India-hosted alternative if required.
  • Google (Analytics 4, Ads): website analytics and advertising, only after consent.
  • Meta (Pixel): advertising measurement, only after consent.
  • LinkedIn (Insight Tag): advertising measurement, only after consent.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

6. Data retention

  • Enquiry and lead records: 12 months from the date of enquiry
  • Enrolment records: 7 years (required for GST compliance under Indian law)
  • Payment records: 7 years (RBI and GST requirements)
  • Analytics data: as per Google Analytics 4 retention settings (14 months by default)

7. Your rights under the DPDP Act

As a Data Principal under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access: request a summary of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Withdraw consent: withdraw your consent to marketing communications or analytics at any time
  • Grievance redressal: raise a complaint about how we have handled your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at neoproed@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and tracking technologies for analytics and advertising. These are activated only after you give explicit consent via the cookie banner displayed on your first visit. If you choose "Essential only", no advertising or analytics cookies are set.

You can withdraw cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or by contacting us.

9. Children's data

This website is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted data through our website, please contact us immediately at neoproed@gmail.com.

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data. Payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay — NeoProEd does not store card numbers, bank credentials, or UPI PINs. All data transmitted to and from our website is encrypted using HTTPS.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve or as legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update. Significant changes will be communicated to enrolled students by email.
